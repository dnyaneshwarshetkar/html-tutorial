Assignment 1: Basic HTML Structure

Create a simple HTML document with the following structure:

Title: "My First Webpage"
Headings:
Main heading: "Welcome to My Website"
Subheading: "About Me"
Paragraphs:
Write a brief introduction about yourself under the "About Me" subheading.
Image:
Insert an image of your choice.
Links:
Include a link to your favorite website.
List:
Create an unordered list with at least three items about your hobbies or interests.
Footer:
Include your name and the current year.
